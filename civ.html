Civilization

Civilization games allow a player to build an entire city and society. The can span many different times in history and technological level, while some even support thr abiloity to increase your technological age as you advance your technology. They can encompass anything a city would need from supplying food, water, textiles, government support, such as plumbing, firefighting, a military, and more.
