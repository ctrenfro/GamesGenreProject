RPG's

Rpg stands for role playing game and puts the player in control of characters in a fictional setting. These games are centered around a narrative with story driven elements. These games also emphasize development of the character as the story progresses.These games can come in many forms of story-telling and gameplay.

Final Fantasy Franchise

Dragon Quest

The Legend Of Heroes

CRPG's

These games have a very different set of rules when it comes to gameplay. Combat is usually set on top-down checkered board similar to a chess or checkers board. Although, they do not display as a checkered board as they are usually represented with a setting such as a town or wilderness setting with a board superimposed upon it. These games also usually allow for a greater amount of character manipulation in regards to a characters background, stats, attributes, and personality.

Wasteland

Fallout 1 & 2

Fire Emblem

MMO's

MMO stands for massively multiplayer online game and urges players to share the narrative provided by the game to be played with others. These games usually have mechanics that require cooperation with other players and the creation of an online community centered around the game.

Final Fantasy 14

World of Warcraft

Action RPG's

These games take story based gamed and encapsulate them in an action packed experience. Real time combat is an emphasis and may come in forms of a hack and slash or an fps.

Diablo

Dark Souls

Ghost of Tsushima
